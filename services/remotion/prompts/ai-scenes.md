# Higgsfield AI Scene Generation Prompts

These prompts can be used with Higgsfield.ai to generate additional scenes if needed.

## Professional Interview Scene

**Use Case:** Alternative background for Scene 3 or Scene 4

**Prompt:**
```
Warm, professional school counselor office with modern furniture and natural lighting through windows. Welcoming atmosphere with plants, comfortable seating, educational posters on walls. Soft focus, high quality, 4K, cinematic lighting, professional interior design, calming color palette with blues and greens.
```

**Settings:**
- Model: Kling 2.6
- Duration: 5 seconds
- Style: Realistic, professional
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9

---

## Notes

- All prompts are optimized for 16:9 aspect ratio (1920x1080)
- Recommended duration: 5 seconds minimum
- Use Kling 2.6 for best quality
- Can use reference images from existing assets for style matching
- Generated videos should be placed in `/remotion/assets/` folder
- Update scene components to use new assets as needed
